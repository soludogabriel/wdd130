History

From the outset, Lit's Raft was built upon the principles of safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship. The founders envisioned not just a rafting company but a community that fosters a deep appreciation for nature's wonders. Early Challenges: The initial years were not without challenges. Lit's Raft faced hurdles in obtaining permits, building a reputable team of guides, and establishing a network of loyal clients. However, the founders' dedication and commitment allowed the company to overcome these obstacles.
